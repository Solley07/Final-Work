About us

Geodata Nigeria

The Geodata Group consists of five key commercial and professional interests in

  • * Real Estate and Civil Engineering
  • * Agriculture
  • * Value Added Industrialization
  • * Mining
  • * Information and Communications Technology
Though the group is making in-roads into investments in Education, Banking and finance, Medicine/ wellness and hospitals, its flagship investments lay at the core of Real Estate and Civil Engineering works by way of professional Turnkey services.

Our Story

The Geodata Group is at the fore of building automated smart but cost friendly homes so as to cater to a growing client base of tech savvy persons who want to live with more ease in making the home comfortable. Our design teams work to from the design stage, plan homes that look to the future. Our homes plan for all forms of future retrofitting and upgrades and we make this plan from the get go.

Our Vision

As an organisation, we see a future where the planet survives because we its inhabitants take proactive measure to ensure this happens and it flourishes. In our quest to do this, we have adopted global best practices whilst building our homes, in the most eco-friendly ways; we spare the earth all the toxic waste that comes with excessive mechanised agriculture, pull out what resources we only need when we mine, and transact all our businesses in the fairest form humanity should have.

Our Mission

To be a world rated organisation of people who redefine professionalism.

Building your smart and luxurious homes.
